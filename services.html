Fibre

Business and Residential Fibre Internet Access is a data and voice solution which provides businesses and homes with high performance and reliable Internet connectivity over fibre.

There are compelling advantages to using fibre over other traditional connectivity methods, i.e. high capacity, symmetrical upload and download speeds and low latency. Fibre is considered by many as an end-game technology, and by choosing Business Fibre Internet Access, customers are taking another step towards future-proofing their businesses.

Broadband Internet

Fixed wireless broadband is a way of connecting to an internet service provider using radio signals rather than cables. This kind of internet is best suited to areas where there are no fibre or cable lines. If you live in an outlying area and need wireless internet for a home without a phone line, this could be a good option for you.

ADSL (an Asymmetric Digital Subscriber Line) uses copper cables to connect you to the Internet. The real advantage of ADSL is that it is available in most areas.
